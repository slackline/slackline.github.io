Planning on hiking the Pennine Way from Kirk Yetholm to Edale.

* Travel

Getting to Kirk Yetholm will involve a train and bus journey from Sheffield to Berwick-upon-Tweed from there two buses
can be caught to get to Kirk Yetholm via Kelso ([[https://www.bordersbuses.co.uk/services/BORD/67][67 to Kelso]] ~ 1h; 81/81A on to Kirk Yetholm).

Checking [[https://www.trainline.com][Trainline]] suggests there is an 09:21 train arriving at 12:21 (no changes) at £66.50, could perhaps get the
09:32 train (one change) arriving at the same time for £50.20.

Checking the 67 bus [[https://passenger-line-assets.s3.eu-west-1.amazonaws.com/bordersbuses/BORD/67-timetable-20220627-d2f17f76.pdf][67 to Kelso]] I'm going to be limited to a bus at 13:00, 15:00 or later. Might be worth getting to
Berwick late on the Tuesday if trains are conducive (e.g. 19:21 from Sheffield arriving at 22:38 then a cheap hostel if
possible which is unlikely as the YHA is £80 minimum!).
