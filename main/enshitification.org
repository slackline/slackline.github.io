#+TITLE: Enshitification
#+FILETAGS: :enshitification:software:services:internet:

Coined by [[https://pluralistic.net/][Cory Doctrow]] it refers to the degradtion of services over time as companies start with a decent product and
then have to repay their investors and turn a profit, ignoring the users who made them successful in the first place.
