#+TITLE: OSMAnd
#+FILETAGS: :foss:osm:android:

[[https://osmand.net][OpenStreetMap Advanced Navigation Device (OSMAnd)]] is a mobile interface to [[id:0a5f391b-0f61-48d8-a4c3-a204d55538de][OpenStreetMap]]

* Privacy

OSMAnd uses telematics to keep track of some aspects, this is enabled when you [[https://fosstodon.org/@ai6yr@m.ai6yr.org/114215691886378067][download maps]]. They use it to "/monitor
fair usage, predict traffic patterns and generate anonymous monthly map reports/". To disable it go to /Settings > OsmAnd
Settings > Identifiers/ and toggle the option.
