** Converting

Ideal tool for extracting audio from videos or converting between different formats.

*** Wave to MP3

Convert a ~.wav~ to ~.mp3~ at 44.1kHz and 320kbps.

#+begin_src bash
ffmpeg -i <input>.wav -vn -ar 44100 -ac 2 -b:a 320k <output>.mp3
#+end_src
