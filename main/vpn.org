#+TITLE: VPN
#+FILETAGS: :vpn:privacy:tracking:

Virtual Private Networks (VPN) allow you to connect to remote networks and all your network traffic passes through it
encrypted and away from prying eyes. Ideal for reducing [[id:a120ecf3-70bb-46e7-9709-37dc59db883c][Tracking]] and improving privacy.

* Wireguard

Wireguard is a [[id:0e6300c6-7025-4f45-820d-4d9da82b41a6][Linux]] kernel level VPN. I installed and configured it on my VPS and personal derices.

** TorVPN

[[https://support.torproject.org/tor-vpn/][TorVPN]] routes data through the Tor network. Still in beta as of writing.
