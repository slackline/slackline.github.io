* ~duf~

~duf~ for a nice alternative to ~du~.


* ~lsd~

A modern version of ~ls~ with coloured output and file details by default.

* ~rmlint~

[[https://github.com/sahib/rmlint][rmlint]] is a fast alternative to find and remove duplicates from your filesystem as well as broken symbolic links.
