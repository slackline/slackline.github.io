* ~duf~

~duf~ for a nice alternative to ~du~.

* [[https://github.com/sharkdp/bat][~bat~]]

An alternative to ~cat~ which is syntax-aware and generally faster.

** [[https://github.com/eth-p/bat-extras][~bat-extras~]]

Extends ~bat~ with additional functionality such as leveraging ~ripgrep~ for searching and ~prettier~ for pretty
printing.


* ~lsd~

A modern version of ~ls~ with coloured output and file details by default.
