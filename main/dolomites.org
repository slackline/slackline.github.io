#+TITLE: Dolomites
#+FILETAGS: :climbing:rockclimbing:italy:dolomites:


I love climbing in the Dolomites, it's my favourite place to climb and so far I've had fourt trips there, all with my
good friend Ben.

* 2011

Met up with Dan to climb the Vinatzer route on Third Sella tower.

+ [[https://www.ukclimbing.com/logbook/crags/col_dei_bos-3177/alvera-134386][Alvera (** V+), Col dei Bos, Falzarego Pass]]
+ [[https://www.ukclimbing.com/logbook/crags/sella_towers-2631/tissi-68906][Tissi (* VI+), First Sella Tower, Sella Pass]]

* 2013

Drove down with Chris as well and Owen met us. I fell off downclimbing the first pitch of the Messner (*** VI) route on
the second Sella tower and fractured my heel, forcing rest, missing out on the route, but after a couple of days just
got on with climbing.

+ [[https://www.ukclimbing.com/logbook/crags/piz_ciavazes-3948/little_micheluzzi-69381][Little Micheluzzi (*** V-), Piz Ciavazes, Sella Pass]]
+ [[https://www.ukclimbing.com/logbook/crags/piz_ciavazes-3948/rossi-162215][Rossi (** IV), Piz Ciavazes, Sella Pass]]
+ [[https://www.ukclimbing.com/logbook/crags/piz_ciavazes-3948/south-west_diedre_-_vinatzer-106701][South-West Diedre/Vinatzer (TD ***), Piz Ciavazes, Sella Pass]]

* 2016

Brilliant trip, our hardest routes and first visit to the Pale region which is amazing. Bit of an epic descending
Frisch/Corradini after dark but rest and a walk up to the Pradidali hut and the brilliant Fissure Buhl was a great
finish to the trip.

+ [[https://www.ukclimbing.com/logbook/crags/falzarego_towers-3178/dibona-136298][Dibona (** V+), Falzarego Towers]]

+ [[https://www.ukclimbing.com/logbook/crags/cima_canali-23157/buhlriss-418292][Fissure Buhl/Buhlriss (*** TD-), Cima Canali, Pale]]
