I had to manually download the [[https://github.com/TypQxQ/Sigenergy-Local-Modbus][Sigenergy-Local-Modbus]] extract the tarball and copy files to
~/var/libs/hass/custom-components~

#+begin_example
cd /tmp/
wget https://github.com/TypQxQ/Sigenergy-Local-Modbus/archive/refs/tags/v.1.2.2.tar.gz
tar xzvf v1.2.2.tar.gz
mv Sigenergy-LocalModbus-v1.2.2/custom_components/sigen /var/lib/hass/custom_components/.
systemctl restart home-assistant.service
#+end_example
