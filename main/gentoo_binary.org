#+TITLE: Gentoo Binary
#+FILETAGS: :gentoo:linux:

In [[https://www.gentoo.org/news/2023/12/29/Gentoo-binary.html][2023]] Gentoo announced that they would be providing binaries for all packages meaning you no longer needed to spend
your own time and energy compiling packages from source.
